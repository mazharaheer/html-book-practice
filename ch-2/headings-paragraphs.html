BRANCHES OF PHYSICS

Mechanics:

It deals with the motion of objects with or without reference of force.

Electricity:

It is concerned with the phenomena and effects related to electric charges

Atomic physics:

It is concerned with the structer and properties of the atom.

Astro physics:
It is concerned with the study of the physics of astronomical bodies.

Nuclear physics:
This branch is concerned with the structer , properties and reactions of the nuclie atoms.
